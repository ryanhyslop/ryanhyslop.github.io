Moving to Octopress

Jul 28th, 2013

Having watched by blog go a bit stale I needed something to re-invigorate my writing process.

My old blog was “Powered By Wordpress” and recently I’ve not been working with that platform often, and find the experience when I do dip into it a bit; deep breath urgh.

Now thats not a criticism of Wordpress at all, its I find myself wanting ALL THE PLUGINS and spend time tinkering on bits that I just don’t want to be doing (PHP mainly).

I discovered Jekyll a while ago and have been keeping my eye on Octopress and have decided to move over and take the plunge. With it being a static site generator I feel its perfectly suited to a fronted development blog as it lets me focus on content, markup and performance, the cornerstones of frontend development.

Also being able to write blog posts in Ai Writer in markdown is awesome sauce.

Hopefully the outcome will be me writing more and being encouraged to slowly iterate on my personal site.

I will try and document my progress and review my experience at a later date.
